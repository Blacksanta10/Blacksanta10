I have been watching anime for about 10 years now. I wanted to start reading the manga because there were LOTS of anime that we stopped short or bad. I now have a finacial situation that allows me start collecting and reading! I am honestly super excited about it. Right now this website is going to be used to document my journey with manga. As time goes on I will add things concerning anime too!

Manga Series starting

I am starting to read a combination of manga that does and doesn't have anime. Here they are.

  1. Rosario and vampire

    This is one of the first anime adaptation I watched. I am excited to read this, because I heard it went on a bit longer than the anime.

  2. Banana Fish

    This does not have an anime adaptation! I found this interesting story through the Vizmedia website. I believe the genre consists of mystery, which is a nice change for me.

  3. Demon slayer

    I watched the first season and the recent movie of demon slayer. After I watched the movie I was overly excited about the upcoming seasons. I decided I did not want to wait though, so I am jumping right into the manga starting after the Mugen train.
